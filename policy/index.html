OpenEd Labs — Training Engagement & Trial Policy

Effective date: June 1, 2026
Website: https://openedlabs.eduspace.work

This document defines how OpenEd Labs delivers trial training sessions (online or offline) and how training engagement contracts are created when a client/institute chooses to continue.

1) Trainer Models

  • Online Trainers: Deliver demo and/or live sessions remotely.
  • Offline Trainers: Visit the institute and deliver demo/trial sessions in person.

2) Trial Session Model (No Upfront Charges)

No upfront charges: OpenEd Labs does not charge any upfront fees for delivering trial sessions, whether delivered online or offline.

  • Online trainers conduct sessions remotely.
  • Offline trainers conduct sessions at the institute location.

3) Trial Completion and Decision

After trial sessions, the client/institute may decide whether to continue engagement with OpenEd Labs and the assigned trainer(s).

4) Engagement Contract (Post-Trial)

If the client/institute chooses to continue:

  • Contract type: Engagement will be formalized as either Part-time or Freelance, depending on the agreed structure.
  • Contract terms: The contract will specify engagement duration, session frequency/timings, training scope (modules/topics and outcomes), and responsibilities of OpenEd Labs, trainer(s), and the client/institute.
  • Standard process: All engagement creation follows OpenEd Labs’ standard onboarding and contracting process.

6) Roles and Responsibilities

  • OpenEd Labs: Coordinates scheduling, onboarding, trial delivery, and contract documentation.
  • Trainer (Online/Offline): Delivers training sessions and maintains session quality per the agreed scope.
  • Client/Institute/Participant: Provides required access/resources (where applicable), confirms attendance/scheduling for continued engagement, and collaborates on training needs.

7) Professional Conduct

Trainers and participants are expected to maintain professional conduct during trial and contracted sessions. Any changes to schedule, scope, or responsibilities must be agreed through the engagement process.

8) Acceptance of Policy

By participating in trial sessions and/or proceeding to a contracted engagement, the client/institute and trainer(s) agree to this policy.

9) Document Control

This policy is effective from the date it is issued by OpenEd Labs and applies to all trials and engagements conducted under this model.
